FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer both local and international shipping. For Sri Lanka, we provide free shipping on orders over 10,000 LKR. International shipping rates vary by destination. We also offer in-store pickup for your convenience.

How long does delivery take?

Local deliveries within Colombo typically take 2-3 business days. Other areas in Sri Lanka may take 3-5 business days. International deliveries can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Please refer to our Return & Exchange Policy page for complete details.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or through the courier's website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders. Online payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

How do I care for my saree?

Care instructions vary by fabric. Handwoven and silk sarees should be dry cleaned. Cotton sarees can be hand washed in cold water. Always check the care label on your garment for specific instructions.

Do you offer customization services?

Yes, we offer customization for certain items. Please contact us directly to discuss your requirements, timeline, and pricing for custom orders.

How can I find the right size?

Please refer to our Size Guide page for detailed measurements. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer service team who can help you choose the right size.
