RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY

Your satisfaction is our priority

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All original tags and packaging intact
  • Accompanied by original receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items without original tags
  • Damaged items due to misuse

How to Return

  1. Contact us at info@fmlk.lk or call +94 11 234 5678 within 14 days of receiving your order
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We will provide you with return instructions and a return authorization number
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item back to us or drop it off at our store

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to your original payment method
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective or incorrect

Exchange Policy

We gladly accept exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item, subject to availability.

How to Exchange

  1. Contact us within 14 days of delivery
  2. Specify the item you wish to exchange and your preferred size/color
  3. Follow the return process above
  4. We will ship your replacement once we receive your return

Exchange Shipping

  • Free exchange shipping for size/color exchanges (Sri Lanka only)
  • Customer pays return shipping; we cover shipping for the replacement item

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.

Contact Us

For any questions about returns or exchanges, please contact our customer service team:

  • Email: info@fmlk.lk
  • Phone: +94 11 234 5678
  • Store Hours: Mon-Sat 9AM-7PM, Sun 10AM-5PM
