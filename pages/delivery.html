Shipping Information

Local Delivery (Sri Lanka)

We offer island-wide delivery across Sri Lanka through our trusted courier partners.

  • Colombo Metro Area: 2-3 business days
  • Other Areas: 3-5 business days
  • Free Shipping: Available on orders over 10,000 LKR
  • Standard Shipping Fee: 500 LKR for orders under 10,000 LKR

International Delivery

We ship to select countries worldwide. Delivery times and costs vary by destination.

  • Asia: 7-10 business days
  • Europe, USA, Canada, Australia: 10-14 business days
  • Shipping rates: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight

In-Store Pickup

Prefer to pick up your order? You can collect it from our store at no additional cost!

  • Select "Store Pickup" at checkout
  • You'll receive a notification when your order is ready
  • Bring your order confirmation and ID
  • Available during store hours: Mon-Sat 9AM-7PM, Sun 10AM-5PM

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your shipment using this number on our website or the courier's website.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact us immediately at info@fmlk.lk or call +94 11 234 5678. We will work with our courier partners to resolve the issue promptly.

Important Notes

  • Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by courier services or customs clearance
  • International customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes
